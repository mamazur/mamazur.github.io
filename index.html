I'm Maria,

a full-stack software developer with a history of working in the financial services and online advertising industry and a Master's degree focused in Informatics

I am currently working as a software developer in a SCRUM team responsible for digitalizing a lending process for business customers at one of the most renovated German banks. My team is developing an in-house cloud hosted application using mostly Vue.js and Java.
In the past few years I have worked mostly with dockerized application written in Node.js, Vue.js, Ruby on Rails and Elixir on AWS infrastructure.

I have also gathered experience in large enterprise projects while working as technical consultant in the area of customer communication services, as well as a development consultant for SAP projects creating custom tailored web based user portals.

Having worked for various companies with international teams have shown me a crucial importance of open and proactive communication for the successful completion of a project. In addition to technical knowledge, I consider myself to be a skilled communicator and collaborator. I enjoy working closely with clients and team members to understand their needs and to develop custom solutions that meet their unique requirements, especially incorporating principles of Lean UX.
