Free Art - for Freedom
We are artists, web designers, creators and (IT) engineers. We live in Germany, but many of us have friends and family in Ukraine. Together with many others we try to support people in Ukraine by volunteering, donating to accommodating refugees etc. But also we do, what we do best - we create and want to share our art.
