I'm Maria.

I am a full-stack web developer with over 5 years of experience in the industry and Masters Degree in Computer Science from the RheinMain University of applied science.
I am currently working as a software developer in a SCRUM team responsible for shipping an enterprise solution for contextual advertising. My team is developing an in-house cloud hosted concurrent application using Elixir and Phoenix.
In the past few years I have worked mostly with dockerized application written in Node.js, Ruby on Rails, Vue.js and Elixir on AWS infrustructure.

I have also gathered experience in large enterprise projects while working as technical consultant in the area of customer communication services, as well as a development consultant for SAP projects creating custom tailored web based user portals.

Having worked for various companies with international teams have shown me a crucial importance of open and proactive communication for the successful completion of a project. In addition to technical knowledge, I consider myself to be a skilled communicator and collaborator. I enjoy working closely with clients and team members to understand their needs and to develop custom solutions that meet their unique requirements, especially incorporating a principles of Lean UX.
